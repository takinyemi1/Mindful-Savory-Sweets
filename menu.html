Appetizers


Bang Bang Shrimp

Crispy, fried shrimp tossed in a creamy, spicy sauce and served with cocktail sauce.

Nachos

Nachos are loaded with seasoned beef, guacamole, pico de gallo, bacon, and green jalepeno peppers.

Angus Beef Sliders

Beef Sliders are served in threes and made classic diner style.

Each slider is topped with a mayo and ketchup spread, three slices of yellow, American cheese, onions, lettuce, another slice of cheese, and another spread of mayo and ketchup.



Garlic Bread

Garlic bread served in fives and has a smooth garlic butter spread, topped with grated parmesan and mozzarella cheese, and served with marinara sauce.


Boneless Chicken Wings

Chicken wings are tossed in a sweet thai chilli sauce, and are typically served with ranch or honey mustard.

Shrimp Cocktail

Jumbo shrimp tossed in a classic shrimp cocktail sauce and served in a bed of lettuce.

Teriyaki Skewers

Chicken marinated in teriyaki sauce and grilled to perfection.

Sesame seeds will be sprinkled on top, along with fresh parsely and cilantro. These can also be entrees with the option of being served over steamed white rice or fried rice.



Mozzarella Sticks

Deep fried with melted cheese inside and served with a marinara dipping sauce.

Entrees


Baked Fish Tacos

Made with crispy, baked fish and topped with green jalepeno peppers, grated parmesan cheese, and a cilantro-lime coleslaw.

Chicken Tikka Masala

An Indian curry dish that is tomato based, seasoned with garlic, ginger, and fenugreek leaves that leave a minty aftertaste.

Chicken is topped with cilantro and served with rice and either garlic naan or cheese naan.



Pasta Primavera

Pasta is seasoned with salt and pepper and is tossed with a vegetable mixture, including cherry tomatoes, that has been seasoned with oil, salt, pepper, and dried herbs.

Tri-Tip Steak

Pan roasted steak, topped with green asparagus, oyster mushrooms, cherry tomatoes, and italian parsely, chimichurri and ficelle crostini.


Sandwiches


Philly Cheesesteak

Sandwich composed of thinly sliced steak, coated in melted cheese, sauteed onions, and sometimes fajita veggies.

Each bite is packed with a savory flavor, impact guaranteed to last.



Colossal Sandwich

Sandwich composed of avocado, ham, bacon, lettuce, tomatoes, and two slightly melted American cheese slices, usually served within toasted bread.

BLT

A known classic, served with bacon, lettuce, cheese, and tomato, with a mayo spread on toasted bread.

Bondiola Sandwich

An Argentinan classic sandwich that is composed of thick slices of roasted pork shoukder and served on top of toasted brioche bread slices.


Chicken Fettuccine Alfredo

Pasta should be creamy, smooth, and topped with 6 slices of chicken breasts either cut into halves or cubes.

Cast Iron Pizza

A pizza that should be made in a cast-iron skillet and returned with crispy edges and a browned crust. Toppings should vary, with our most commonly used ones being pepperoni, sausage, grilled chicken, mushrooms, meatballs.


Salads


Caesar Salad

A staple salad, served with greens, croutons, and cheese, and sauced in a creamy dressing of any choice, most usually caesar dressing.

Cobb Salad

Another staple salad that is composed of lettuce and greens, topped with bacon, shredded cheese, sliced boiled eggs, corn, tomatoes, with an optional choice of chicken, and is usually dressed with avocado lime dressing.

The chicken the salad can be topped with varies between fried chicken, grilled, chicken, hot-grilled chicken, cold-grilled chicken, spicy-grilled chicken, and so much more!



Greek Salad

A salad composed of fresh cucumber, peppers, tomatoes, and red onions, sauced with a vinaigrette dressing that is often composed of garlic, oregano, mustard, vinegar, and salt.

Panzanella

A refreshing Italian salad composed of stale bread, tomatoes, and cucumbers, along with other fresh vegetables.

It's typically sauced in a vinaigrette dressing of either light balsamic vinaigrette or apple cider vinaigrette.



Desserts


Profiteroles

A French choux pastry ball that can be filled with either custard or ice cream, and is then drizzled with chocolate.

Millionaire's Shortbread

A shortbread bar that is layered with a buttery shortbread, a thick caramel layer, and topped with a dark chocolate spread that has an optional topping of sea salt.

Cannoli

A classic Italian dessert.

It consists of a fried pastry dough that can be filled with cream cheese by itself or cream cheese topped with chocolate chips on the ends, or they can even be drizzled with chocolate.



Baklava

A Greek delicacy that consists of layers of crispy phyllo dough, honey, and nuts.

The nuts vary from chopped pistachios, walnuts, or hazelnuts.




Cheesecake Brownie

These cheesecake brownies consist of the usual chocolate brownie mix.

They are then be topped with cream cheese, that have various designs made on them.



Tiramisu

A classic no-bake Italian dessert that consists of coffee-soaked ladyfingers, a creamy mascarpone custard, topped with whipped cream and a dust of cocoa powder.

Cinnamon Rolls

A fluffy, soft dessert.

It consists of a cinnamon brown sugar filling, glazed on top with vanilla cream cheese.



Red Velvet Cake

A moist and flavorful cake dessert that consists of red velvet flavored cake layers with fluffy cream cheese frosting filled in between each layer.
